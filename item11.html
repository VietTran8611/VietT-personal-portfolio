MERN Elearning Template

LMS website made with MERN

I just want a place to store all education youtube playlist that I watched through out the year and inspire by other youtubers selling course I made a learning management system to store those playlists as courses

The authentication is from my mern auth template here. The LMS itself has filteres fetching and keep track of which courses you own and which do not. It has order, and cart functionality like a Ecomerce website but I don't do payment cause I do not sell anything. Progression and completion of a course is being kept track of
